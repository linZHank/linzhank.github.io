As an animal living on Earth, I have various goals: fill my starving belly, win a soccer game, become a paleontologist, etc.. Most (if not all) of my actions and behaviors are based on these goals. Of course, some of my actions bring me toward my goals while others take me away. Theoretically, I can improve my performance by practicing again and again, unless my goal is only determined by luck (e.g. lottery). This is the process of reinforcement learning: set a goal, performance actions, improve actions from feedback.
